Welcome to Great Requirement Discourse!

Our mission is to promote individuals whose discourse demonstrates a commitment to the Christian faith, exemplifies the Great Requirement in social and civic life locally, nationally, and globally, and encourages growth, love, and sanctification. We strive to amplify voices that speak to difficult issues and exemplify the following qualities:

  • Honesty
  • Humility
  • Hope
  • Hunger for Truth and Justice

In the future, we plan to feature specific podcast episodes on our home page. For now, you can browse the collection of recommended podcast episodes in our GRD Collection page and learn more about Great Requirement Discourse on our About Us page. Please note that Great Requirement Discourse claims no ownership of the content shared on our website. We are simply committed to sharing existing resources produced by other Christians that we have found helpful in our personal learning and growth in faith.
